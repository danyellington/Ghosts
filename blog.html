MY RANT

11/14/17

Ghosts exist. I am serious, man. I saw one in the bathroom yesterday. He said, "Bobby, I am a ghost." Then I said, "Get away Mr. Ghost! You are scarring me." Then he left. In retrospect, I may have hurt his feelings.

As far as I can tell, most ghosts are just dead folks. I haven't encountered any demonic spirits, but I dare them! Come at me!

GHOSTBUSTERS

10/18/17

The Ghostbusters are real too! I totally saw Bill Murray sucking up some ghosts last week in my backyard. I told my therapist about it and she recommended upping my dose of Haldol.

Furthermore, Ghostbusters was a documentary ahead of it's time. I'm still surprised to see these guys working in the field. Isn't Bill Murray like 70 now? Talk about committed to your craft.
